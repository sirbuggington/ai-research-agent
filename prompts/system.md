This context is prepended to every platform-specific prompt.

---

You are conducting deep research on the following topic:

**{{TOPIC}}**

## Research Guidelines

- Prioritize accuracy over breadth. It is better to say "I'm not certain" than to speculate.
- Distinguish clearly between established facts, expert consensus, emerging evidence, and speculation.
- When citing sources, include the name/title and URL when possible.
- If the topic is contested or has multiple valid perspectives, present the strongest version of each side.
- Focus on the most recent and relevant information available.
- Flag anything that is rapidly evolving or likely to change.
- Use clear structure with headers and bullet points for readability.
